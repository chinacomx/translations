梦中，冰夫人见神仙座下有仙女纵身一跃，那仙女生得玲珑模样，却眼中噙泪，令冰夫人怜爱不已，遂伸手揽仙女入怀。

<--->

In her dream, Madam Bing saw a divine maiden leap down from the seat of an immortal. The maiden had a delicate face, but tears welled in her eyes. Madam Bing felt great compassion and reached out to hold the maiden in her arms.
