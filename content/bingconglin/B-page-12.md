- 但天不遂人愿，冰家长女生下后久病不愈，夭折于襁褓。痛失爱女的冰夫人日日在送子观音像前虔诚祭拜。
- However, things don't turn out the way people wish. The eldest daughter of the Bing family fell ill shortly after birth and passed away in infancy. Grieving the loss of her beloved daughter, a heartbroken Madam Bing prayed and offered sacrifices every day before the statue of the Child-Giving Guanyin.
