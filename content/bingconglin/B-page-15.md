九月后的建昌夜，一声婴孩哭响从冰府传出，随即惊雷骤雨从天而降——大地久旱逢甘霖，冰府也终于迎来新生。

<--->

One night, nine months later, in Jianchang City, a baby’s cry rang out from the Bing residence. Thunder rumbled, followed immediately by a sudden downpour. After a long drought, the land was finally blessed with rain, and at the same time the Bing family welcomed new life.
