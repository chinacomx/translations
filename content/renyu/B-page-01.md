- 一&emsp;宽广的海洋，富饶美丽。它蕴藏着多少神奇的秘密，象珍珠一样放出异彩，召唤着人们去探求。人鱼的传说，就是其中的一个。
- 1&emsp;The vast ocean, abundant and beautiful. It emits a radiant splendor like that of a pearl, summoning people to explore its many miraculous secrets. The legend of the mermaid is one among them. 

{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}

- 二&emsp;人鱼，古今中外都有过记载。公元前三世纪巴比伦的巴罗索斯在《古代历史》一书中这样描述：她的身体像鱼，尾部长着人一样的双脚。她有天赋的理性，引导人通晓一切，还使人优雅和善良。
- 2&emsp;Mermaids: there are written accounts about them from the past until the present, in China and abroad. In the third century BC, Borassus of Babylon gave the following description in his Ancient History: “Her body resembles a fish, with her tail extending into two human-like feet. She has the gift of reason, guiding people to understand everything thoroughly, and she also commands people to act gracefully and kindheartedly.” 

{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}

- 三&emsp;十七世纪伦敦出版的《赫特生航海日志》一书中也对人鱼做过描写，说她象一个女人，皮肤洁白，肩上披着长长的头发，潜水的时候，能清楚地看到她那灵巧的尾巴。
- 3&emsp;Also in the Hudson Logbook, published in the seventeenth century in London, there is a description of a mermaid: she resembles a woman with pure white skin and long hair cascading over her shoulders; whenever she plunges into the sea, one can clearly see her agile tail.

{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}

- 四&emsp;另外，在欧洲维斯杜拉河畔，还流传着有关人鱼的传说。那时，河里有一头水妖，到处害人。人鱼为了维护正义，英勇地与水妖搏斗。最后终于用华尔那沙为她铸造的一把剑征服了水妖。
- 4&emsp;In addition, on the banks of the Vistula River in Europe, there is another legend passed down about a mermaid. At the time, there was a water demon in the river, bringing harm to people all around. A mermaid, with the purpose of restoring justice, heroically struggled with the water demon. Ultimately, she vanquished the demon using a double-edged sword forged for her by Wars. 

{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}

-  五&emsp;于是人们就用华尔那沙的名字命名了河边的一座城市，这就是今天的波兰首都“华沙”，并以人鱼的像做为华沙的城徽。如今，一座手执剑和盾的美人鱼铜像还屹立在维斯拉河岸呢！
-  5&emsp;Thereupon, people used the name Wars for the town located by the side of the river, and this hence is the capital of today’s Poland, Warsaw. Moreover, the image of a mermaid became the city’s coat of arms. Until today, a bronze statue of a sword-and-shield-bearing mermaid still stands tall on Vistula’s river bank!

{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}

-  六&emsp;更令人惊异的事情是发生在1830年，伦敦一国博物馆里竟然展出了一件人鱼标本，有人介绍说这是在苏伊士东部的一条河边捉到的。参观的人络绎不绝。
-  6&emsp;An even more astonishing thing happened in the year 1830. In a national museum in London unexpectedly a mermaid specimen was put on display. Someone described its origin as being caught by a river in the eastern part of Suez. There was an endless stream of visitors.
