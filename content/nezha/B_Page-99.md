99 李靖被哪吒赶得上天无路，入地无门。正在危急之际，只见山岗上有一道人倚松靠石而立。那道人言道：“快上岗来，站在我后面，待我救你。”

- 
99&emsp;Li Jing fled in desperation, unable to find the path to heaven and the gateway into the earth. In this moment of peril, he saw a Daoist standing by a pine tree on a hill, leaning against a rock. The Daoist called out: “Quick, come up here and stand behind me—I will save you.”
