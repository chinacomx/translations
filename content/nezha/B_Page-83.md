- 83&emsp;哪吒性急，将枪挑开剑，用手取金砖往空中打来。木吒不提防，一砖正中后心，跌在地下。
- 83&emsp;Hot-tempered, Nezha swept aside the sword with his spear, then seized the golden brick from his pouch and hurled it into the air. Caught off guard, Muzha was struck squarely in the middle of the back and tumbled to the ground.
