(18)程海跑到马路对面，刚才那两个人已不在了。他四周看了一下，踱到窗口下，仰着头问老伴：“他们哪里去了？”老伴说：“没看清。”

<--->

(18) Cheng Hai ran across the road, [but] the two people were already gone. He looked around, [then] walked over to the window and, raising his head, asked his wife, “Where did they go?” She replied, “[I] didn’t see clearly.”
