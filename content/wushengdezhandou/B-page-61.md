(61) However, the most vicious dog of a landlord refused Cheng Hai’s family a foothold in the village, forcing them to take temporary refuge in a cave. Up on the mountain, the wind was blowing mercilessly. Inside the cave, next to Cheng Hai, who was all covered in cuts and bruises, lay his only daughter, who wasn’t even a year old.
