二老爷走到跟前故意说：“你也是个最爱为人民服务的好社员，怎么不下河帮着救呢？”姥姥焦急地说：“光有好心，没本事也白搭呀！”“没本事就练吧！”姥姥说：“二兄弟，你别逗我了，那有老太太学游泳的呀！”二老爷说：“你不能学，就该让国青好好学呀！”姥姥不由的叹口气：“渴了挖井不赶趟，让他现使现练也来不及啦！”

Great Uncle walked up and said teasingly: “You are a good people’s commune member who loves serving the people most. So why don’t you jump into the river and save the mule?” Grandma replied anxiously: “It’s no good just having a kind heart. If you don’t have the skills, it’s useless!” Great Uncle said: “Then go learn the skills if you don’t have them!” Grandma answered: “Brother, don’t tease me. How could an old woman like me learn to swim now?” Great Uncle said: “If you can’t learn, then let Guoqing learn properly!” Grandma sighed and muttered: “It’s too late to dig a well when you’re already thirsty, and it’s too late for him to start practicing right now!”
