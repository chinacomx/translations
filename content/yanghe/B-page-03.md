第二天，国青和几个小伙伴跟着社员们到果园里摘果子。姥姥知道了，又把他拉回家说：“你没干过这活计，爬树登高，容易出危险。做别的事儿吧。”

<--->

On the second day, Guoqing and some pals followed the people’s commune members to the orchard to pick fruit. When his grandma found out, she brought him back home again and said: “You’ve never done this kind of work before. Climbing trees high can easily lead to danger. Why don’t you find something else to do?”
