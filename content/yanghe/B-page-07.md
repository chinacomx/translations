- 二老爷对姥姥说：“你疼外孙子，可是办法不大对头哇！”说着，走过来拉住国青的小手：“走，我领你去见见世面。”姥姥赶忙说：“去吧，跟二老爷去，我放心。”
- Second Great Uncle said to the grandma: “You love your grandson, but the way you’re going about it isn’t quite right.” Then he walked over and took Guoqing’s small hand. “Come on, kid,” he said, “let me show you the world.” Grandma quickly said: “Go ahead! When you are with your Great Uncle, I feel much better.”
