姥姥急得又搓手，又跺脚，回头一看跑来了二老爷，就喊：“二兄弟，快帮这位同志救救落水的骡驹子吧！”

<--->

Grandma was so anxious that she rubbed her hands and stomped her feet. Just then, she turned around and saw the Second Great Uncle running toward them. She called out: “Brother, hurry! Help this comrade save the mule from drowning!”
