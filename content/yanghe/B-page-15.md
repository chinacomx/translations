- 二老爷带着游泳小组的小朋友们，绕个弯子，来到小树林后面的浅河滩。他说：“孩子们，毛主席号召到大江大海里锻炼，你们要听毛主席的话。来，我教你们！”孩子们高兴极了，“稀里哗啦”地跳进河里，跟在二老爷后面学打扑通，学扎猛子，好象大鸭子带着一群小鸭子。
- Second Great Uncle led the kids from the swim team around a bend to a shallow part of the river behind a grove of trees. He said: “Kids, Chairman Mao tells us to train ourselves in big rivers and seas, and you should listen to Chairman Mao. Come on, I’ll teach you!” “Xi-li hua-la!” The children were thrilled and jumped into the water. They followed Great Uncle and learned how to kick and dive, just like a big duck leading a bunch of little ducklings.
