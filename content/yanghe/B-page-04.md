- 第三天，国青孤零零地呆在家里，闷极啦。托着下巴颏坐在台阶上，两只眼直愣愣地看着蜜蜂在豆架上飞，听着小鸟在枝头上叫……
- On the third day, Guoqing stayed home all by himself, and he was bored out of his mind. Resting his chin in his hands, he sat on the front step, staring blankly as bees buzzed around the bean trellis and listening to the little birds chirping in the trees...
