姥姥看花了眼；二老爷抿着嘴地笑；那个壮年人拍手直叫好。国青把骡驹子救上岸，脸不红，气不喘，真是呱呱叫！

Grandma was amazed; Great-uncle grinned; that man clapped his hands and cheered loudly. Guoqing helped the little mule safely onto the riverbank with his face calm, not even out of breath. He was absolutely amazing!
