这当儿，河岸高梁地里一阵哗哗响，蹿出一个小男孩。姥姥一眼认出那是她的宝贝国青，就连声喊：“哎呀，你怎么弄得一身水？”国青二话没说，一纵身，“扑通”跳进水里，没影了。

<--->

Just then, there was a sudden rustle from the sorghum field by the riverbank, and out dashed a little boy. Grandma instantly recognized her precious Guoqing and called out again and again: “Oh my! Why are you soaking wet?” But Guoqing didn’t say a word. He took one big leap and jumped into the water. “Pu-tong!” He disappeared.
