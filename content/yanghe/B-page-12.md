有一天，刚吃过午饭，国青就不见了，姥姥到街上找，村外喊，找到洋河边上，远远看见一群孩子正在往河里跳，赶紧跑过去，瞧见国青正要脱裤子下河。

<--->

One day, just after lunch, Guoqing suddenly disappeared. Grandma searched the street, called out of the village, and finally reached the banks of the Yang River. From a distance, she saw a group of kids jumping into the water. She hurried over and saw Guoqing was just about to take off his pants and go to the river.
