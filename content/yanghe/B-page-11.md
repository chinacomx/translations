打这儿起，人们发现国青变了：又跟着大车拉黄土，又到果园里摘果子。姥姥要拉他回家，他理直气壮地说：“我要练胆大！我要学本领！”

<--->

From that day on, people found that Guoqing had changed. He helped haul yellow earth with the big carts again, and also went back to the orchard to pick fruit. When his grandma tried to bring him back home, he declared proudly: “I want to train myself to be brave! I want to learn skills!”
