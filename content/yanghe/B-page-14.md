姥姥急得正没办法，忽见树林里走出二老爷。二老爷对姥姥说：“你快回家干活儿吧，孩子的事儿我来管。”姥姥说：“有你带着他们我就放心了。”

<--->

Grandma was getting really worried and didn’t know what to do. Suddenly, Second Great Uncle appeared from the woods. He said to her: “You can go home and get back to your work. I’ll take care of the kids.” Grandma replied: “As long as they’re with you, I don’t worry.”
