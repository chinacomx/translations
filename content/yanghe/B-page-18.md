- 糟糕，小骡驹子掉到河里了。姥姥急着说：“快下河捞吧！”壮年人说：“唉，我不会凫水！”姥姥生气地说：“这么大的人连凫水都不会？”那人说：“我从小没学过……”
- Oh no, the little mule had fallen into the river! Grandma cried out: “Quick, get in the river and pull it out!” But the man answered: “Ugh, I don’t know how to swim!” Grandma retorted in a huff: “A grown man like you, how come you can’t swim?” The man said: “When I was a child, I never learned it...”
