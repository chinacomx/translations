国青一见姥姥，就一边躲闪一边说：“我要学游泳，锻炼身体，长大了保卫祖国！”姥姥喊：“乖乖，你还小，等长大一点再学吧。”国青一听急了眼：“胆子得从小练，本领得从小学；我们游泳小组互相帮助，您甭担心！”

<--->

As soon as Guoqing saw his grandma, he started to dodge her and said: “I want to learn how to swim, to exercise, so when I grow up, I can protect our country!” Grandma called out: “Oh, dear, you’re still too young. Why don’t you wait with that until you're a bit older?” But Guoqing grew anxious and blurted: “Building courage should start from a young age! Training skills should start from a young age! We are a swim team to help each other. You don’t need to worry!”
