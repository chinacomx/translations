- 姥姥出来进去的紧忙乎：一会儿帮着饲养场簸豆料，一会儿又帮着运输队补套包。姥姥瞅见国青噘着嘴巴不高兴，就问他是为啥。国青没好气地说：“你总把我关在家里！”姥姥说：“你要什么我都给，到外边乱闯，我可不放心。”
- Grandma was bustling in and out, busy as ever: sifting soybean feed at the breeding farm one moment, and helping the transport team fix sacks the next. When she saw Guoqing sitting there with his lips all puckered, clearly upset, she asked for the reason. Guoqing replied grumpily: “You’re always keeping me stuck at home!” Grandma said: “I can give you everything you need, but if you run around outside, I just can’t help worrying.”
