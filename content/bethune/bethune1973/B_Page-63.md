(63) During a frontline battle, the medical convoy’s supply cart got blocked by fallen boulders. Just as Bethune grew frantic, a local farmer came trotting past with a little donkey. His eyes lit up at the sight of the “dung carrier ” on its back of the donkey. “Ah! Here’s our solution!” he exclaimed.

// The “dung carrier ”: A distinctive donkey harness used in North China villages—willow-woven saddle baskets traditionally for transporting animal dung or manure, though adaptable for other loads. //
