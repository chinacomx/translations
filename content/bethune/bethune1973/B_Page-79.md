(79) Bethune had an immense love for the people and often patrolled different villages with his medical kit on his back. On one occasion, an elderly woman rushed out from the crowd and said urgently, “Doctor Bai, my daughter-in-law is gravely ill. She has been crying out in pain day and night, is wasted to skin and bones. We’ve consulted many doctors, but none of them could cure her...”
