（116）白求恩听了报告，镇定地问：“还有多少需要动手术的伤员？”同志们回答：“十个，大都是重伤员。”

(116) After listening to the report, Bethune calmly asked, “How many more wounded need operations?” “Ten, mostly severely injured.” the comrades replied.
