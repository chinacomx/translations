（109）童秘书走近问：“有什么新闻？”白求恩指着桌上一些书报说：“慕尼黑协定！……马德里危在旦夕！蒋介石出卖中国人民整个世界发了邪！这家杂志要我写一篇稿子，要多一点浪漫气息，见他的鬼！”

(109) Secretary Tong approached and asked: “Any news?” Bethune pointed to the books and newspapers on the table: “The Munich Agreement! ... Madrid is on the brink of collapse! Chiang Kai-shek is betraying the Chinese people—the whole world has gone mad! This magazine wants me to write an article, adding some more romantic flair—damn them!”
