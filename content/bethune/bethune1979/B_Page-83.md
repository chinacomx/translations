（83）深山小路上，模范医院留守人员和老乡们正抬着担架，把伤员往山里撤。小虎走在担架的前面，忽见山下不远处，一队日本兵，牵着猎犬，正一步一步逼上山来。

(83) On a narrow mountain path, the remaining staff of the model hospital and local villagers were carrying stretchers, evacuating the wounded deeper into the mountains. Xiaohu walked ahead of the stretchers when suddenly, not far down the slope, he spotted a group of Japanese soldiers with hunting dogs, slowly advancing up the hill.
