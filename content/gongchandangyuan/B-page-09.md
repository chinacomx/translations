- 46&emsp;刘大山起身告辞，出了大门，回头看见白帆在玲玲搀扶下站在门口，正向自己这边望呢。刘大山的心一动，“哦，他不是块榆木疙瘩。”
- 46&emsp;Liu Dashan got up to take his leave, went out the door, turned his head, and saw Bai Fan standing at the doorway supported by Lingling and looking right his way. Liu Dashan’s heart stirred: “Oh, he’s not a wooden blockhead after all.”

- 47&emsp;夜深了，刘大山走在宽阔的大道上，仿佛再次听到老师长的吼声：“你是共产党员吗？”他觉得，这问话每一个共产党员都应当回答。
- 47&emsp;Deep into the night, Liu Dashan walked along a broad avenue, as if hearing the old division commander’s roar once again: “Are you a Communist?” He felt this is a question every Communist Party member ought to answer.
