{{% columns %}}
- 28&emsp;吕久才说：“你盼我年轻是不可能，你应该想办法叫青年人早成熟五年、十年，那是完全办得到的。”
- 28&emsp;Lü Jiucai said: “Your wish that I was younger is impossible—you should think of ways to make young people mature five or ten years earlier, that is totally achievable.”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 29&emsp;晚上，刘大山回到家，老伴责问他干吗给老白处分。他把缸子使劲一敦：“婆娘不要参预政事。婆娘参政，非捅漏子不可！”
- 29&emsp;In the evening, Liu Dashan returned home, and His wife demanded to know why he gave Old Bai a disciplinary action. He slammed down his mug with force: “Woman, don’t partake in political affairs. Womenfolk participating in politics, that can only end in trouble!”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 30&emsp;老伴又说：“不行！你得给老白活动活动。” 刘大山眉毛唰地一下立起来了：“活动个屁，是我建议党委处分他的！”
- 30&emsp;His wife continued: “Won’t do! You must pull some strings for Old Bai.” Liu Dashan’s eyebrows shot up: “Pull strings, my ass! I’m the one who suggested disciplinary action to the Party Committee!”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 31&emsp;正在刘大山夫妻俩说话时，敲门进来一个三十多岁的青年人，低着头，手里拎着个很大的兜子。
- 31&emsp;While Liu Dashan and his spouse were talking, a young man in his thirties knocked at the door and came in, with his head lowered, carrying a large canvas bag in his hand.
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 32&emsp;那人见了刘大山，呜呜哭起来，一问，是白塔站站长。刘大山说：“你是哭站长职位丢了哩！你哭不回来，俺不是慈面菩萨！”
- 32&emsp;When the man saw Liu Dashan, he started sobbing loudly. When asked, he said he was the Baita stationmaster. Liu Dashan said: “You’re only crying because you lost the stationmaster position! You can’t cry it back, I ain’t no benevolent Bodhisattva!”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 33&emsp;青年人哭声没了，眼睛飞快地瞥了刘大山一眼，嘴角掠过一丝不以为然的笑：“刘局长，你这么干下去，以后……”
- 33&emsp;The young man’s weeping sound ceased, his razor-sharp eyes glimpsed at Liu Dashan, and a disapproving smirk flickered across his lips: “Bureau chief Liu, if you keep this up, in the future…”
{{% /columns %}}
