{{% columns %}}
- 22&emsp;机车鸣叫，打断了他的思索。眼前是设备陈旧的枢纽站。他想：落后啊！中国的落后就是共产党员的耻辱！一个分局长居然带头……
- 22&emsp;A locomotive whistled, interrupting his train of thoughts. Before him was a hub station with outdated equipment. He thought: “How backward! It is China’s backwardness that is the shame of Communists! And a branch office chief actually took the lead in this…”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 23&emsp;他抓起电话：“老白嘛？我说你改行吧，唱戏去！你是个好演员，假的说成真的。你啥时候学的这一套？这叫共产党蒙哄共产党！……”
- 23&emsp;He grabbed the phone: “Old Bai? I say you should change your profession, go perform in theatre! You’re a good actor, talking falsehoods into truths. When did you learn this trick? This is called the Communist Party fooling the Communist Party!...”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 24&emsp;电话里白帆又争辩又哀求。刘大山把电话咣当一下撂在桌上，在屋里走了一圈，又抓起话筒：“你还是不是共产党员？你等着接受处分！”
- 24&emsp;On the phone, Bai Fan both argued and pleaded. Liu Dashan slammed the phone on the desk, circled the room, and picked up the receiver again: “Are you still a Communist or not? You just wait for your disciplinary action!”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 25&emsp;当天晚上，局党委会通过了刘大山的提议：给白帆记大过一次，撤销其安全监察主任职务；撤销白塔车站站长职务；通报表彰吕久才。
- 25&emsp;That evening, the Bureau Party Committee passed Liu Dashan’s proposal: to note down Bai Fan’s serious mistake of the first degree and to remove him from his post as head of safety oversight; to remove the Baita stationmaster from his post; and to issue a circular commending Lü Jiucai.
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 26&emsp;开完会，他感到疲倦，刚坐稳，就进来一个人，当他知道来人就是吕久才时，深情地问：“你是共产党员吗？” “是，五〇年入党。”
- 26&emsp;After the meeting ended, he felt tired. Just as he sat down, a person came in. When he realized this person was Lü Jiucai, he asked with deep feeling: “Are you a Communist?” “Yes, I joined the Party in 1950.”
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 27&emsp;沉默了一会，他突然抱住老人，眼里闪着泪光：“老伙计，你真是好样的，可惜五十九岁了，要是年轻几岁多好，很多岗位需要你这样忠诚的人。”
- 27&emsp;After a moment of silence, he suddenly embraced the old man, with tears glistening in his eyes: “Old partner, you are a truly good person. It’s a pity you’re fifty-nine. If you were just a few years younger, so many posts could be filled with a loyal person like you.”
{{% /columns %}}
