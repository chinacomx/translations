{{% columns %}}
- 1&emsp;铁路局办公室的秘书们为了给复职上任的局长刘大山布置办公室，真是绞尽了脑汁。但刘大山只待了一天就搬到靠车站的那个小房间。
- 1&emsp;The railway bureau secretaries truly racked their brains in setting up the office for reinstated bureau chief Liu Dashan. But Liu Dashan only stayed there for a day and immediately moved to the small room by the train station.
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 2&emsp;这里，整个车站尽收眼底。调度员的说话声，装卸工的喊叫声，检车员的敲击声，不时吹进刘大山耳朵里。他大嘴一咧笑了。
- 2&emsp;From here, the entire station was in full panoramic view. The chatter of the dispatchers, the shouts of the dockers, and the pounding and tapping of the wagon inspectors frequently blasted into Liu Dashan’s ears. He broke into a wide, open-mouthed grin.
{{% /columns %}}

{{% columns %}}
- 3&emsp;局长搬家的事传开了。人们自然回忆起他以前的一些事：在他个子还没有小马枪高的时候，就参加了游击队，打鬼子，跟国民党干。
- 3&emsp;The news about the bureau chief moving out of the office spread. People naturally recalled some things from his past: when he was not even as tall as a rifle, he had joined the guerrilla forces to fight the Japanese devils and against the Guomindang.
{{% /columns %}}
